Do we have a tagline?

Cormorant Interactive is a small independent studio created as part of Algonquin College's Game Development Program.

Formed in September 2025, our team of 13 hard at work on 'Honk! Ranger Rush', a third person roguelike that blends fast paced combat with an environmentally conscious message.

Something’s fishy in the Canadian Wildlife Services, and you’re going to find out what!

Take a gander at the shenanigans your park’s geese are getting up to and uncover their nefarious plot before it’s too late. Travel coast to coast to defeat their armies before the whole country gets too loosey-goosey!
